About

Vishak Marcil

My work explores the tension between elegance and honesty. I shoot portraits, fashion, weddings, and nature with a calm, editorial eye, shaping each frame through an intentional, film-inspired grade.

As an editor, I focus on rhythm, texture, and atmosphere—crafting a visual language that feels tactile and true. From intimate weddings to stylized fashion stories, I build imagery that lingers.
